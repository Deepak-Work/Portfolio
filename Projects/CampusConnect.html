Campus Connect

A full-stack centralized hub for university clubs and their members

Summary

CampusConnect is an innovative web application designed to streamline communication and collaboration among university clubs and their members. Built using Django and SQLite, this platform serves as a dynamic hub for aggregating newsletters, facilitating efficient communication, and fostering a sense of community within the campus. Unify offers a range of features including centralized announcement sharing, real-time member engagement, personalized notification settings, and an archival system for past communications. The application employs a robust user role system, allowing for Club Administrators, Club Government members, and regular Club Members, each with tailored functionalities. CampusConnect addresses key challenges such as information fragmentation, limited interactivity, and event coordination difficulties. By providing a centralized space for information exchange and collaboration, CampusConnect simplifies club management and enhances the overall university experience for both club leaders and members alike.
