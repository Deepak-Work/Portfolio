Foreign Whispers

Convert YouTube video to speech and written subtitles in German, cloning speaker's voices.

Summary

This project focuses on the conversion of YouTube videos into speech with synchronized subtitles in German, all while maintaining the natural voices of the original speakers. The technological framework relies on the Whisper package developed by OpenAI to transcribe audio into text. A pre-trained translation model, trained on the Opus dataset, facilitates the conversion of English text into German. Subsequently, a Text-to-Speech (TTS) module is utilized to transform the translated text into speech. To ensure seamless synchronization between audio and video, batch processing is employed. Additionally, FFmpeg is utilized to embed the translated subtitles directly into the video stream. These functionalities are seamlessly integrated into an accessible online application, streamlining the process for users.
